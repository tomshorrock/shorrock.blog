10:30 - PAK win toss and bat

First thing to note in this warm-up match is that both teams are allowed to use their full 15-man squad throughout the match.

English pitches are being prepared to be good for batting. I wouldn’t expect it to be any different this time. The runs line is around 325 although liquidity is a problem.
